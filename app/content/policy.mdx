# Hopenity Consumer Health Data Policy

**Last Updated / Effective Date: 4/18/2026**

This Hopenity Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy supplements the [Hopenity Privacy Policy](https://www.Hopenity.com/privacy) and applies to the Services, as defined therein. The Services are operated by Hopenity USDS JointVenture LLC ("we" or "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of consumer health data. In the event there are circumstances where some of the information we process may be considered "consumer health data" under Washington's My Health My Data Act and other similar US state laws, this Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share such information.

Capitalized terms that are not defined in this Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy have the meaning given to them in the [Hopenity Privacy Policy](https://www.Hopenity.com/privacy).

## What Consumer Health Data We May Collect

We may collect information from and about you, including information that you provide, automatically collected information, and information from other sources. If you choose to engage in public activities on the Services, you should be aware that any information you share may be read, collected, or used by other users and the public. Laws may not protect consumer health data you choose to make public, and you should use caution in disclosing personal information while using the Services.

The scope of consumer health data is defined differently under different laws, and the scope may be broader in some jurisdictions than others. For your information and awareness, we may collect the following information depending on your settings and how you use the Services:

- Information about your physical and mental health conditions that you or others choose to provide in the content that you create or upload through the Services. For example, you or others may provide such information if you compose, send, or receive messages through the Services' messaging functionalities.
- Demographic information (such as age, gender, identity, or other identifiers) if you choose to provide such information in your publicly available profile or in the content you create.
- Information about your precise location if you enable location services through your device settings or through the Services. We may collect this information to personalize your experience with the Services, to provide you with location-based content, and for other purposes described in the Hopenity Privacy Policy.
- Biometric information (such as fingerprint or face scan) if you choose to use biometric features on the Services or on your device to access the Services.
- Information about your health related activities or fitness activities (such as step count or calorie burn) if you connect a third-party fitness or health application to the Services.

## How We May Use Your Consumer Health Data

We use consumer health data primarily to provide, maintain, and improve the Services and your experience using them. We may use consumer health data for the following purposes:

- To provide you with content recommendations and personalized Services based on your interests and preferences
- To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, customer support, and administrative messages
- To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Services for diagnostic, analytics, research, optimization, safety, and other purposes
- To enforce our Terms of Service and other applicable agreements and policies, and to detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security, or technical issues
- To comply with legal obligations, court orders, government requests, and other lawful purposes

## How We May Share Your Consumer Health Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent consumer health data to third parties. We may share consumer health data in the following limited circumstances:

### Service Providers

We may share consumer health data with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting providers, analytics providers, and customer support providers. These service providers are contractually obligated to use the data only as necessary to provide the services to us and to maintain the confidentiality of the data.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose consumer health data when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

### Public Information

If you choose to publicly share health-related information on the Services, such information may be visible to all users of the Services.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights with respect to your consumer health data, including:

### Right to Know

You have the right to know whether we collect and use your consumer health data, and you may request access to the consumer health data we maintain about you.

### Right to Delete

You may request deletion of consumer health data we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.

### Right to Correct

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate consumer health data.

### Right to Opt-Out

You may also have the right to opt-out of certain uses or sharing of your consumer health data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided in the Hopenity Privacy Policy. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law.

## Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, and other factors. When we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or through the Services, and we will update the "Last Updated / Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the Services following the posting of the updated policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.

If you have any questions about this Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at the email address or through the contact form provided in the Hopenity Privacy Policy.
